Summary card

Use summary cards to provide a short review or recap of form selections before the final submission.

Canada.ca guidance Portal guidance

Canada.ca guidance

Keep summary cards simple and focused on the highlighted information. Use clear headings and a clean stack layout.

Example layout

<section class="summary-card">
  <h3>Review your answers</h3>
  <dl>
    <dt>Name</dt>
    <dd>Jane Doe</dd>
    <dt>Application type</dt>
    <dd>Renewal</dd>
  </dl>
</section>

Portal guidance

Inside portals, summary cards may include a slightly stronger panel style or border treatment to match portal visuals.

Example layout

<section class="summary-card portal-style">
  <h3>Review your answers</h3>
  <dl>
    <dt>Name</dt>
    <dd>Jane Doe</dd>
    <dt>Application type</dt>
    <dd>Renewal</dd>
  </dl>
</section>
