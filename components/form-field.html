Form field

Design accessible form fields with clear labels, help text, and error states.

Canada.ca guidance Portal guidance

Canada.ca guidance

Use left-aligned labels and consistent spacing for readability. Keep help text and error text close to the input.

Example markup

<label for="email">Email address</label>
<input id="email" name="email" aria-describedby="email-help email-error" />
<p id="email-help">We use this address for notifications.</p>
<p id="email-error" class="error">Enter a valid email address.</p>

Portal guidance

Inside portals, use the portal layout and field grouping rules. Confirm whether fields should appear inside framed cards or clean panels.

Example markup

<div class="portal-field-group">
  <label for="email">Email address</label>
  <input id="email" name="email" aria-describedby="email-help email-error" />
  <p id="email-help">We use this address for notifications.</p>
  <p id="email-error" class="error">Enter a valid email address.</p>
</div>
